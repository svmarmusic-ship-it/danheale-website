About

More than a decade
on the decks.

Dan Heale has been a fixture in the North East club scene for over ten years, with a style rooted in the warmth and groove of classic house and the hypnotic depth of modern deep house. His sets draw on a wide-ranging knowledge of dance music history and a sharp ear for what works on a dancefloor.

Alongside his DJ career, Dan is a professional music producer with over 100 million streams to his name — a background that informs every mix with a producer's understanding of sound, energy, and arrangement. He's held residencies across the North East and played alongside internationally recognised DJs and acts.

Available for club nights, festivals, private events, and residencies across the UK and Europe.
